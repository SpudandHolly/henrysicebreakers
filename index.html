Henry's Icebreakers

A companion to Be a Better Boss by Henry Engelhardt, who built one of the best places to work anywhere by treating enjoying the work and winning at it as the same thing.

Before every meeting, shake for a would-you-rather scenario. One question, no right answer—just a moment to help your team think differently and discuss openly. 300 dilemmas across five tiers, from Friday-meeting fun to the calls that quietly shape a culture.

In the spirit of the book, whose profits support the Moondance Foundation.
